1. Concession_id relationship is a one-to-many as a customer is allowed to purchase one or multiple type of concession items
2. Employee_id relationship between employee and ticket table is a one-to-many as an employee can sell multiple tickets
3. The relationship between movie and customer is a one-to-many as one movie can have multiple customer watching
4. The relationship between movie and ticket is a one-to-one as one movie will have one type of ticket id, this does not determine the amount of tickets is for one type of movie
5. The relationship between ticket and customer is a one-to-one as a customer will have one ticket id
